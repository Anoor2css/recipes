Meal type: Dessert
Prepare time: 15 minutes
Cook time: 20 minutes
Level: low
Yield: 5 servings
Ingredients:

1) 250g unblanched whole almonds;
2) 150g milk chocolate, 150g dark chocolate, break into small pieces.

Directions:

1: Preheat oven to 300F. Toast almonds on a baking sheet for 12 minutes (Picture 1). Set aside to cool.
2. Place chocolate in a 7-in stainless-steel bowl (Picture 2-3). Place bowl over simmering water (Picture 4) until chocolate has melted.
